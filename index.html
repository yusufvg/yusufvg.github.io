Education

For the past 3.5 years, I was a Computer Science major at the University of Maryland, College Park. I first found my passion for coding in middle school, and was hooked after creating my first HTML file. Since then, I have been working non-stop to ensure that I am always expanding my horizons and never stop learning. Though I graduated with a B.S. in the winter of 2020, that goal of perpetual learning hasn't stopped and will continue into my next chapter.

Work Experience

With regards to professional experience, I have worked with Na Ali'i as a Development Intern and shadowed their full stack teams working on their Navy Payroll overhaul contract. Prior to that, I was an IT Lab Intern at NIST, leading development on an audio utility for their virtual reality environment. In addition, I worked with iD Tech several times as a Lead and Private instructor to help ensure that the next generation of coders and tinkerers has access to the resources and skills they need to realize their wildest projects.

About Me

Outside of my time spent programming, I played club volleyball at UMD and was an Alaka'i (Cultural Teacher) at E Ala E Hawaiian Cultural Center. I still play competitive volleyball to this day, and the perpetuation and protection of kānaka maoli (the Hawaiian people) culture is something very near and dear to my heart. I also really enjoy swimming and reading fantasy and sci fi.
