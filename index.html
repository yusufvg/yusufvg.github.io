Education

From 2017-2020, I studied Computer Science major at the University of Maryland, College Park. I first found my passion for coding in middle school, and was hooked after creating my first HTML file. Since then, I have been working non-stop to ensure that I am always expanding my horizons and never stop learning. Though I graduated with a B.S. in the winter of 2020, that goal of perpetual learning hasn't stopped and will continue into my next chapter.

Work Experience

Since December 2021, I have been working as a SWE at Google, on both FE and BE projects. Prior to that, I have worked with Na Ali'i as a Development Intern and shadowed their full stack teams working on their Navy Payroll overhaul contract. Prior to that, I was an IT Lab Intern at NIST, leading development on an audio utility for their virtual reality environment. In addition, I worked with iD Tech several times as a Lead and Private instructor to help ensure that the next generation of coders and tinkerers has access to the resources and skills they need to realize their wildest projects.

About Me

Outside of my time spent programming, I played club volleyball at UMD and was an Alaka'i (Cultural Teacher) at E Ala E Hawaiian Cultural Center. I still play competitive volleyball to this day, and the perpetuation and protection of kānaka maoli (the Hawaiian people) culture is something very near and dear to my heart. I also really enjoy swimming and reading fantasy and sci fi.

My Portfolio

Kōnane Online

A Flutter/Firebase app for the Hawai'ian game Kōnane. The game is played by jumping over an opponent's piece to capture it, akin to checkers, and a player wins when their opponent has no possible moves left. The game supports playing local over the table, vs a computer, and online against friends.

Metro Pass Calculator

WMATA's commuter pass program is a bit complicated and confusing. This simple web app built with Vite+Tailwind lets you easily log your usual trips and automatically calculate which tier of commuter pass saves you the most money!

Open Gym Queue

Little Battle Planet
